Add your mirror's redirect URI to Google Cloud Console

This one-time setup allows your mirror to authenticate with Google via your phone's browser.

  1. 1
    Open Google Cloud Console
    Go to console.cloud.google.com on your computer or phone.
    Sign in with the Google account that owns the project.
  2. 2
    Open Credentials
    In the left sidebar: APIs & Services → Credentials
  3. 3
    Click your OAuth 2.0 Client ID
    You'll see it under "OAuth 2.0 Client IDs". Click the name or edit (✏) icon.
  4. 4
    Add the redirect URI
    Under Authorized redirect URIs, click + ADD URI.
    Paste the URI shown above and press Enter.
    The URI must match exactly — no trailing slash.
  5. 5
    Save
    Click the blue Save button at the bottom of the page.
    Changes take effect within a few seconds.
  6. 6
    Return to setup and sign in
    Go back to the Mira setup page and click Sign in with Google.
